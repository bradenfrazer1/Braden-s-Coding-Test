Lead-Up

As early as Britian had sent us colonists over, we were under a "hands off" policy in wich as long as we gave Britian money, they got to do as they wished. We were not under taxing, and we got to do as we wished. When the French and Indian War Came along, we were getting destroyed by the French, so we got help from the British. They sent soldiers and trained us so that we could win the war. This caused the British to go into serious debt. They decided to use things like the "Stamp Act"(all paper needed to have a government made stamp in order for it to be legal, this stamp was expensive). We absolutely hated this and after several more taxes like this, we threw a whole lot of tea into the Boston Harbor. These acts led to the first battle of the american revolution!
